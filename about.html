A game is a fun activity with rules that can be mental or physical, and is played for entertainment.
Games can be played with different types of equipment, such as controllers, playing cards, or the body. The goal of a game can vary, and some games have simple rules, while others have complicated rules.
Games are sometimes played purely for enjoyment, sometimes for achievement or reward as well. They can be played alone, in teams, or online; by amateurs or by professionals. The players may have an audience of non-players, such as when people are entertained by watching a chess championship.
